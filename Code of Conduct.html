Code of Conduct for CipherCraft Hackathon

Introduction

At CipherCraft Hackathon, we are dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or technology choices. We do not tolerate harassment of participants in any form.

Expected Behavior

  • Respect: Treat everyone with respect and consideration. Value each otherâ€™s ideas, styles, and viewpoints.
  • Inclusivity: Be inclusive and collaborative. Encourage and support fellow participants.
  • Communication: Communicate openly and thoughtfully, without being disruptive or causing harm.
  • Integrity: Demonstrate honesty and integrity in your interactions and work. Give credit where it's due and avoid plagiarism.
  • Environmental Responsibility: Be mindful of the environment. Dispose of waste properly and make efforts to minimize your ecological footprint.

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Harassment: Harassment includes offensive verbal comments, deliberate intimidation, stalking, following, harassing photography or recording, sustained disruption of talks or other events, inappropriate physical contact, and unwelcome sexual attention.
  • Discrimination: Discrimination or any kind of exclusionary behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Violence: Any threats or acts of violence are strictly prohibited.
  • Sabotage: Deliberate sabotage of other participants' work or equipment is prohibited.

Unique Points

  • Intellectual Property: Respect the intellectual property rights of others. Ensure that your projects and ideas are original or properly credited.
  • Collaboration Encouragement: While competition is a part of the hackathon, we strongly encourage collaboration and mutual support. Share knowledge and help others succeed.
  • Community Building: Engage in building a positive and supportive community. Network and connect with peers, mentors, and organizers.
  • Mental Health Awareness: Be mindful of your own and others' mental health. Take breaks, get enough rest, and encourage a healthy work-life balance.

Reporting

If you are being harassed, notice that someone else is being harassed, or have any other concerns, please contact a member of the hackathon staff immediately. We are here to help and will take appropriate action to address the issue.

Consequences

Participants asked to stop any harassing behavior are expected to comply immediately. If a participant engages in harassing behavior, the hackathon organizers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning the offender or expulsion from the event.

Commitment

We value your attendance and want everyone to have an enjoyable and fulfilling experience. As such, all attendees, sponsors, partners, volunteers, and staff at CipherCraft Hackathon are required to agree with the code of conduct. Organizers will enforce this code throughout the event.

Thank you for helping make this a welcoming, friendly, and innovative event for all.
